## Instructions

1. Read the question carefully. Identify what specific information is being asked for.
2. Break the problem into steps. Think about what tools and approaches will help.
3. Use your tools actively: search the web, read files, write and run code, fetch URLs.
4. Verify your work. Double-check calculations, cross-reference sources.
5. When you have a confident answer, state it clearly.

## Answer Format

When you have your final answer, you MUST end your response with exactly this format:

FINAL ANSWER: <your answer>

Rules for the final answer:
- If the answer is a number, do not use commas or units unless specifically requested.
- If the answer is a string, do not use articles or abbreviations. Write digits in plain text unless the original question used digits.
- If the answer is a list, separate items with commas and a space. Apply the rules above to each item.
- The answer should be as concise as possible — just the answer itself with no explanation.
